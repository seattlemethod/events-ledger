# Business Event

ISO/IEC:
Occurrence in time that partners (3.51) to a business transaction (3.8) wish to monitor or control.

REA:
An occurrence in time below the granularity level of an Economic Event that consumes resources, but
which cannot be paried logicall and somewhat immediately with an acquisition or production event that materializes
an identifiable represantable resource. Economic event usually comprise multiple business events, each of whic
can have either a singler assocated time (receive raw materials immediately) or a duration (inspect raw materials
over the course of 10 minutes).  The criteral for recognition of Business Events is the decomposition level to which the system
modeler wishes to plan, control, or evaluate the economic phenomena under study.  If a Business Event
has duration, we automatically assume that it possesses at least two sub-events: an instantaneous starting occurrence and
an instentaneous ending occurrence.

Wikidata:
Undefined

A&AD:
A business event is an ecnomic event that spawns a financial transaction or the need for information related to a financial transaction.
